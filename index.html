Meet Lizzie

Lizzie Buras is a dedicated REALTOR® with Gulf Coast Real Estate, serving Houma and the surrounding Terrebonne Parish area. As a lifelong Louisiana resident and devoted mother of two, she understands what makes our community special and is passionate about helping families find their perfect home in South Louisiana.

Before real estate, Lizzie spent 8 years as a dental assistant, where she honed her skills in patient care, empathy, and honest communication. This foundation of caring for people now defines her approach as a real estate professional – treating every client with the same dedication and attention she would want for her own family.

Whether you're buying your first home, upgrading to something bigger, or looking to sell, Lizzie provides the knowledge, dedication, and caring service that makes all the difference. She's not just your realtor – she's your neighbor, committed to helping you navigate one of life's most important decisions with genuine care and professional excellence.
